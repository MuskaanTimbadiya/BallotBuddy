About Ballot Buddy

Democracy thrives when its citizens are informed and engaged. Ballot Buddy is an initiative designed to simplify the complex election process in India. Whether you are a first-time voter learning how to register, or an experienced citizen verifying your polling booth, this guide provides clear, actionable, and official information.

Our objective is to ensure electoral participation is accessible to all eligible citizens, reinforcing the foundation of the world's largest democracy.
